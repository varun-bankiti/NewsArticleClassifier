Tata Motors  hasn’t had much success with its passenger car business. Despite heavy investments and new car launches, including the world's most affordable car Nano. The company is now making a fresh attempt at reviving its loss-making business. The top launches would include an entry level sedan 'Kite 5' in 2016, followed by a new SUV codenamed 'Eagle' in 2016 and 'X104' in 2017. The company may also be enter the premium segment through an SUV codenamed 'X601' in 2018 and premium sedan in 2019.
